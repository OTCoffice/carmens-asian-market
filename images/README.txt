Drop real product photos here, named by stock code, e.g.:
  F529A.jpg   (Cirio Passata Classica)
  A080B.jpg   (Tilda Basmati 5kg)

Then in index.html, find that product in the PRODUCTS array and add an img field:
  {code:'F529A', brand:'Cirio', name:'Passata Classica', cn:'經典番茄碎醬', ... , img:'images/F529A.jpg'},

The card and cart will automatically use the photo instead of the emoji tile.
Recommended: square-ish JPG/PNG, ~600x480px or larger.
